# Billing dispute policy

## Dispute window

Customers can dispute invoice charges within thirty days of invoice generation. Disputes after thirty days require finance leadership approval before any credit is issued.

The dispute window applies to subscription charges, usage overages, taxes, and support plan fees. Contract termination fees follow the signed order form instead of this policy.

## Support handling

Support should acknowledge a billing dispute within one business day and collect the invoice number, customer account id, disputed amount, and expected correction.

Support may explain the invoice calculation, but cannot promise credits, waive overage charges, or change tax details. Finance owns the final decision.

## Finance review

Finance reviews the dispute, checks usage exports, and decides whether to issue a credit, replacement invoice, or denial. Customer success is included for enterprise accounts with repeated disputes.

## Communication

The customer receives a written decision with the corrected amount or the reason for denial. If a credit is approved, it appears on the next invoice unless finance issues a manual transfer.
