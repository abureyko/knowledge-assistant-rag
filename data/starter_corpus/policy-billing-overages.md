# Usage overage policy

## Thresholds

Customers receive an automated warning at eighty percent and one hundred percent of their contracted usage.

Enterprise customers with custom contracts may receive an additional warning at sixty percent if the order form requests proactive outreach. The warning is sent to billing administrators and copied to the assigned customer success manager.

## Charges

Overage charges are billed at the rate defined in the signed order form and appear on the next monthly invoice.

If the order form does not define an overage rate, the account is flagged for customer success review instead of automatic charging. Support should not quote a rate from another customer contract.

## Escalation

Enterprise accounts with repeated overages must be reviewed by customer success within three business days.

Customer success should check whether the overage is expected growth, accidental usage, or a missing plan upgrade. Finance can pause automatic overage billing for one cycle when customer success opens a documented expansion discussion.

## Disputes

Customers can dispute overage charges within thirty days of invoice generation. Disputes after thirty days require finance leadership approval.
